Socioeconomic
In the first weeks of the pandemic, surgical face masks in Indonesia soared in price by over six times the original retail value from around IDR 30,000 to IDR 185,000 (some sources said it exceeds IDR 300,000) per box in some outlets after the announcement of two citizens tested positive for the coronavirus.[242] Panic buying was reported since mid-February before the first cases were confirmed.[243] President of Indonesia Joko Widodo condemned the hoarding of face masks and hand sanitizers[244] and Indonesian National Police started to crack down on suspected hoarders.[245]
